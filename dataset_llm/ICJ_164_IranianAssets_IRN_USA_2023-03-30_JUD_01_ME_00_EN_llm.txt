      THE COURT,

      composed as above,

      after deliberation,

      delivers the following Judgment:


      1. On 14 June 2016, the Islamic Republic of Iran (hereinafter “Iran”) filed in the Registry of
the Court an Application instituting proceedings against the United States of America (hereinafter
the “United States”) with regard to a dispute concerning alleged violations by the United States of the
Treaty of Amity, Economic Relations, and Consular Rights, which was signed by the two States in
Tehran on 15 August 1955 and entered into force on 16 June 1957 (hereinafter the “Treaty of Amity”
or “Treaty”).
